Kradle

ABOUT

Kradle is a world’s first - a hosted database and business management solution customised by you to meet your requirements. Built around a hierarchical structure that you design, kradle is capable of scaling from a single user to a large organisation with a high number of users and complex access rights. With full file storage and management options and a powerful business process management module, kradle is designed to allow you to take control of your business. kradle’s unique architecture and ease of use empowers the user by offering a database structure specifically designed around your business. kradle puts you in charge. No longer is there a need to bring in database experts, complex schema designs and high fees to make changes as the business grows and evolves. You stay in charge.

FEATURES

ok, what do you get? Fully customisable database structure to match your company. Once the structure is defined, you decide how and where your data is stored. Lightning fast response from our patented blade design will allow you to navigate and access data online quicker than you believed possible. You decide on access and permission control by setting up role based groups. Powerful and comprehensive business process module customised by you for your business. All businesses have processes which are unique. Match kradle’s BPM module to your business, not the other way round. Easy to use reporting module to track how your business is performing. Hosted securely on Microsoft’s proven Azure and SQL platforms.Generous per user data and file storage allowance. Flexible pricing with free trial and upgrade options.
